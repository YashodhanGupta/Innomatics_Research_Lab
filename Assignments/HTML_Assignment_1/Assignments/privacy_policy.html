Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 30-01-2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Personal Budget Tracker. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle the information you enter into the tracker and outlines your rights regarding your data.

2. Information We Collect

The Budget Tracker does not collect, store, or share personal data. However, we may process the following information locally on your device:

  • Income details (amount, date, category)
  • Expense details (amount, date, category)
  • Transaction types (Income or Expense)

All entered data is used solely for displaying budget summaries within the tracker and is not transmitted to any external servers.

3. How Your Information is Used

The data you enter is used only for the following purposes:

  • Displaying income and expenses within the summary table
  • Helping you track financial transactions
  • Providing a simple way to monitor your budget

No data is permanently stored, shared, or analyzed beyond your usage of the tracker.

4. Data Security

Since no data is stored online or transmitted, your financial records remain private. However, to enhance security:

  • If using a shared device, clear your entries before exiting.
  • Avoid entering sensitive financial details (e.g., bank account numbers).

5. Third-Party Services

This tracker does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics, or external APIs. Your data remains private and is processed only within your browser.

6. Cookies and Tracking

This Budget Tracker does not use cookies, tracking scripts, or data collection technologies.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review it periodically.

8. Your Rights

As a user, you have the following rights:

  • The right to control and delete your entered data.
  • The right to use the tracker without the risk of data misuse.
